Az PowerShell Module · AZ-104 · AZ-700 · Find VM sizes, resource SKUs, regions, availability, and quota
Heads up! Get-AzComputeResourceSku is the most reliable way to check actual availability — Get-AzVMSize lists sizes but doesn't account for capacity restrictions in a region. Always check restrictions before deploying.
